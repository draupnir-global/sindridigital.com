SERVICES

Advisory & operational guidance for Bitcoin and digital assets.

When things go wrong

Wallet & Seed Recovery

Technical forensics & reconstruction

Lost seed phrases, corrupted hardware, forgotten passphrases. We deploy forensic techniques—NAND extraction, key recovery algorithms, brute-force optimization—to reconstruct access. Success is not guaranteed. Costs are transparent and fixed in advance.

Forensic Tracing

On-chain investigation & source tracking

We trace stolen or misappropriated Bitcoin across exchanges and mixing services using heuristic analysis, blockchain forensics, and institutional data sources. Output includes timeline maps, wallet clusters, and exchange entry points suitable for law enforcement coordination.

Post-Incident Response

Recovery planning after compromise

When a wallet has been compromised—key theft, exchange account takeover, social engineering—time matters. We stabilize remaining holdings, coordinate with exchanges and law enforcement, and design recovery strategies for partially lost funds.

Knowledge & diligence

Counterparty Due Diligence

Not all institutional Bitcoin services are equivalent. We conduct technical and regulatory due diligence on custody providers, exchanges, and lending platforms. Reports detail security infrastructure, operational procedures, and residual risks.

How we work

The network model

We do not attempt to be a one-stop service. Instead, we coordinate a precision network of specialists—forensic investigators, estate attorneys, custody providers, exchange relationships, and recovery experts. Each brings deep expertise in their domain. We provide the technical orchestration and continuity.

This model has two advantages: you access genuine specialists rather than generalists trying to cover every topic, and you maintain direct relationships with your custody provider and legal advisors. We are the translator between technical capability and business risk.

Our boundaries

We are not FINMA-regulated. We do not hold client assets, provide legal advice, offer AML/KYC services, or act as compliance officers. We do not work with banks, governments, or authorities. We serve private individuals and private companies with legitimate Bitcoin holdings.

We are also not a VQF member and do not participate in Swiss cryptocurrency regulatory frameworks for institutional intermediaries. This is deliberate. Our advisory is technical and operational, not regulatory. If your situation requires a licensed service—a custody provider, an attorney, a compliance officer—we identify the right partner and coordinate the introduction.

For your holdings

Self-Custody Architecture

Technical structure & security

We design operational frameworks for direct Bitcoin ownership—from hardware wallet configuration to multisig structures to institutional custody via regulated providers. Each architecture reflects your specific risk profile, regulatory context, and succession plan.

Inheritance & Estate Planning

Succession & family structures

Bitcoin inheritance requires technical preparation years in advance. We work with family offices and estate planners to structure seed recovery, threshold schemes, and testamentary handoff protocols that survive the loss of any single individual.

Operational Security Review

Vulnerability assessment & hardening

We audit existing Bitcoin holdings for operational weaknesses—hot wallet exposure, seed storage vulnerabilities, access control gaps. Recommendations are implemented in coordination with your IT security team and custody providers.
