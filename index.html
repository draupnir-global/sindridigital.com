SERVICES

What we deliver

We produce institutional-grade blockchain reports. No legal opinions, no financial planning, no custody. We work with certified forensics partners, deliver under our own name, and get paid on completion.

Forensic Tracing

On-chain investigation

We trace stolen or misappropriated Bitcoin across exchanges and mixing services. Output includes timeline maps, wallet clusters, and transaction flows. The client decides how to use the report — in court, with law enforcement, or with their bank.

Source-of-Funds Reports

Institutional-grade verification

We produce source-of-funds documentation that meets the standard required by Swiss banks and regulated custodians. Full chain-of-custody, wallet provenance, and risk scoring — delivered as a branded Sindri Digital report.

Incident Documentation

Evidence packages after theft or loss

When crypto assets are lost or stolen, we produce the forensic evidence package — transaction histories, wallet analysis, flow-of-funds diagrams. Structured for use in legal proceedings, insurance claims, or regulatory filings.

Counterparty Screening

Pre-transaction wallet verification

Before entering a transaction, verify the other side. We screen wallet addresses against sanctions lists, darknet exposure, mixer activity, and known fraud clusters. Clear report, delivered before you commit.

Loss Assessment

Quantification & documentation

We quantify and document crypto losses to the standard required by tax authorities, insurers, and legal counsel. Historical price data, transaction reconstruction, and certified valuation — all in one report.

HOW WE WORK

Built different.

Network model

Specialists, not generalists

We do not try to be everything. For every engagement, we assemble the right team from a network of certified forensics partners, each with deep expertise in their domain. We coordinate, quality-check, and deliver under our name.

Skin in the game

Our own capital on the line

We hold digital assets on our own balance sheet. The same partners, the same processes, and the same standards we use for clients — we use for ourselves. That changes how carefully you work.

Clear boundaries

What we don't do

We are not FINMA-regulated. We do not hold client assets, provide legal opinions, manage portfolios, or act as compliance officers. We produce reports. When a situation requires a licensed provider, we know who to call.

Direct to client

No intermediaries

We work directly with the asset owner — natural persons and private companies. Not banks, not law firms, not government agencies. The client receives the report and decides independently how to use it.

OUR GROUP

Sindri Digital within Draupnir Global

Sindri Digital is the digital asset arm of the Draupnir Global group, headquartered in Zug. We operate our own Bitcoin and digital asset holdings alongside the client-facing forensics practice — separate banking, separate compliance infrastructure, shared precision.

Own holdings

Bitcoin & digital assets

Sindri Digital holds crypto assets on its own balance sheet. Assets received as client payment may be retained in cryptocurrency or converted to fiat — at the company's own discretion and risk. We do not hold client assets.

Infrastructure

Custody & operations

We operate custody infrastructure for the group's digital assets through FINMA-regulated providers. Multisig architecture, cold storage protocols, and disaster recovery — built and maintained in-house for our own holdings.

Part of something larger

Draupnir Global, Zug

Sindri Digital sits within the Draupnir Global group — a Swiss-based holding structure active in commodities, technology, and financial services. The group provides the capital base, the banking relationships, and the operational backbone.
